About

Singapore's Leading Expert in Posture, Spinal Health & Workplace Ergonomics

Dr. Kelvin Ng (Doctor of Chiropractic, USA) is a highly-rated clinician and global speaker with 16+ years of experience. He works with individuals, groups, and organisations to raise awareness of spinal health, posture, and ergonomics — and to correct postural problems through chiropractic adjustments, targeted exercises, and better ergonomic setups.

A native Singaporean, Dr. Ng graduated from Sherman College of Chiropractic, U.S.A. with a Doctor of Chiropractic (Summa Cum Laude) in 2009, winning the prestigious Milton W. Garfunkel Award for superior academic, clinical, and philosophical achievement — and the Academic Achievement Award for the highest GPA in his class.

He is owner and Clinic Director of Family Health Chiropractic Clinic Pte Ltd, a board member of The Chiropractic Association (Singapore), and the author of 2 peer-reviewed research publications.

Posture Rehabilitation

As a Certified Posture Exercise Professional (CPEP), Dr. Kelvin conducts targeted exercises to improve overall posture by working on specific groups of the musculoskeletal system. Highly recommended for those who spend long hours at a desk.

  • Relieves neck and shoulder tension
  • Reduces stiffness from prolonged sitting
  • Activates posture-supporting muscles
  • Decompresses the spine

Ergonomic Assessment

A comprehensive postural ergonomic assessment for individuals and groups — available virtually or on-site. Covers 4 evidence-based components:

1. Workers' Functional Capacity

Biomechanical movement patterns, active sitting, and workspace range of motion.

2. Employees' Posture

Common work-related postural distortion patterns with corrective recommendations and exercises.

3. Brain-Based Needs

Identify factors limiting employee focus, reduce brain fog, and promote a productive working environment.

4. Workplace Environmental

Assess barriers, obstacles, and inefficiencies that compromise safety, cause injury, or limit productivity.

A detailed written report with actionable recommendations is provided at the end of every assessment.
